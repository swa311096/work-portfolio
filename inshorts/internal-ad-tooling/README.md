# Internal Ad Tooling

**Company:** Inshorts ($550M Digital Media Platform)
**Role:** Product Manager, Public App
**Timeline:** Apr 2023 – Jun 2024

## Overview
Scaled Inshorts' internal ad tooling infrastructure to support massive ad distribution across Android and iOS at 2.4 billion monthly impressions.

## Impact
- Served **2.4B monthly Ad impressions** across Android and iOS
- Supported ads from users, news reporters, and brands on a single platform

## What I Did
- Scaled internal ad tooling to handle high-throughput ad delivery
- Built capabilities for distribution, tracking, and measurement of ads
- Enabled three distinct ad supply sources: users, reporters, and brands
