# Content Review Workflow

**Company:** Inshorts ($550M Digital Media Platform)
**Role:** Product Manager, Public App
**Timeline:** Apr 2023 – Jun 2024

## Overview
Built an intelligent content review workflow that prioritized high-risk creators for full review and sampled the rest, integrated with Vertex AI for multilingual video summarization.

## Impact
- Saved **$1M+ annually** in operations costs
- Reduced ops costs by **80%** via AI-powered video summarization

## What I Did
- Designed risk-based prioritization logic to focus reviewer effort on high-risk content
- Integrated Google Vertex AI for multilingual video summarization
- Reduced manual review burden significantly through smart sampling

## Skills & Tools
Content Moderation, AI/ML Integration, Vertex AI, Multilingual NLP, Operations, Risk Prioritization, Cost Reduction, Python
