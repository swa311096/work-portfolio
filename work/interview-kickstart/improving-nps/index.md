# Improving NPS

**Company:** Interview Kickstart (VC-backed EdTech Startup)  
**Role:** Founding Product Manager, New Products & Research  
**Timeline:** Jul 2021 – Mar 2023

## Overview

Led work to raise Net Promoter Score by tightening how we collected learner feedback, prioritized product and operations fixes, and closed the loop with students and internal teams.

## Impact

- Turned NPS signals into a **prioritized backlog** of course and experience improvements  
- Aligned product, instruction, and operations on **shared satisfaction metrics** and regular review  

## What I Did

- Designed and socialized a cadence for reading NPS and qualitative feedback alongside operational data  
- Partnered with instruction and curriculum teams to address recurring detractor themes  
- Worked with GTM and success stakeholders so feedback informed positioning and onboarding, not just the product surface  

## Skills & Tools

NPS, Voice of Customer, Prioritization, EdTech Operations, Cross-functional Leadership, Survey Design, Feedback Loops
