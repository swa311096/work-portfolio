# Pinterest Ads Ecosystem

**Core Signal:** Future-leaning "Planning" intent (home décor, fashion, life events).

**Moat:** High commercial intent in a non-toxic social environment; users "choose" to see products.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Catalog Integration:** Deep integration with visual search and shopability.

**Measurement:** 
Pinterest Ads API provides access to campaign management and analytics, with deep integration for catalogs and visual search.

**Strengths:** 
Unique "planning" signal (early funnel intent); high aesthetic quality and shoppability; users arrive with a mindset to discover and buy.

**Limitations:** 
Best suited for visual/lifestyle categories; scale is smaller than the largest social graphs.

**Primary Use-cases:** 
Home decor, fashion, and lifestyle brand discovery; planning-based performance marketing.
