# Snap Ads Ecosystem

**Core Signal:** Real-world visual communication and AR engagement.

**Moat:** High penetration among Gen Z and leadership in Augmented Reality (AR) advertising.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Direct Response (DR):** Focus with 7-0 and 1-0 attribution models optimized for mobile users.
- **AR Lenses:** Industry leadership in AR-based advertising.

**Measurement:** 
Snapchat's Marketing API enables developers to manage campaigns, audiences, and creative, while reporting on performance data across surfaces.

**Strengths:** 
Leadership in AR creative and performance marketing; high interaction with young demographics; efficient mobile-first conversion optimization.

**Limitations:** 
Niche audience focus (younger skew); overall scale smaller than giants.

**Primary Use-cases:** 
AR-driven brand awareness, mobile-app acquisition, and DTC performance marketing.
