# Criteo Ads Ecosystem

**Core Signal:** Large-scale retailer shopping behavior and commerce audiences.

**Moat:** The Retail Media interface for the "Open Web," connecting brands to hundreds of smaller retailers.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Commerce Audiences:** Powering ads across a vast network of retailers.

**Measurement:** 
Criteo provides detailed commerce-level measurement across its retail partner network.

**Strengths:** 
Primary connector for non-Amazon retail media; deep commerce data on the open web; strong retargeting roots.

**Limitations:** 
Facing identity challenges on the open web (third-party cookie deprecation).

**Primary Use-cases:** 
Retail media scaling, commerce-focused retargeting, and performance display.
