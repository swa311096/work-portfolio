# Meta Ads Ecosystem

**Core Signal:** Social graph, interests, and deep behavioral engagement across Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp.

**Moat:** Advanced lookalike modeling and highly efficient discovery-based ad delivery.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns (ASC):** Automate creative testing and audience targeting. 
- **CAPI (Conversions API):** Heavy reliance on server-side integration to bypass browser-level signal loss and improve attribution.

**Measurement:** 
Focus on "Total Attribution" models that blend 1st party and modeled data. Meta's Conversions API allows server-to-server event sending; Meta provides explicit guidance on deduplicating client (pixel) and server events to prevent double-counting.

**APIs & Partner Ecosystem:** 
Primary buying is through Meta Ads Manager and Business tools. Programmatic creation and management is supported through the Meta Marketing API (campaigns, creatives, and reporting via Insights API). Meta also provides dedicated Advantage+ Shopping Campaign APIs to support automated commerce campaign setup.

**Strengths:** 
Exceptional scale for demand creation and performance marketing driven by rapid creative iteration, broad reach, and strong conversion-optimized delivery when pixel/CAPI signals are reliable.

**Limitations:** 
High sensitivity to privacy changes and signal loss (iOS 14+); reliable server-side/event dedup setups are increasingly important.

**Primary Use-cases:** 
DTC and ecommerce acquisition, prospecting + retargeting, app installs, and broad awareness scaled into conversion optimization via CAPI and automated placements.
