# Walmart Connect Ads Ecosystem

**Core Signal:** Massive brick-and-mortar sales data linked to online accounts.

**Moat:** Dominance of the physical retail landscape in the US.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Omnichannel Measurement:** Linking online ad exposure to in-store register sales.

**Measurement:** 
Walmart Connect provides measurement that links digital exposure to actual store sales data, a key differentiator for FMCG/CPG brands.

**Strengths:** 
Unrivaled physical store data; strong closed-loop measurement for everyday goods; massive national reach in the US.

**Limitations:** 
Primarily relevant for physical-goods brands; ad tech stack is maturing but still trailing Amazon/Google.

**Primary Use-cases:** 
Omnichannel retail growth, CPG product launches, and in-store sales lift.
