# The Trade Desk (TTD)

**Core Signal:** Open internet identity signals (UID2) across CTV, Audio, and Web.

**Moat:** The primary "Neutral" alternative to walled gardens; leader in UID2 identity framework.

**Platform Mechanics:** 
- **Decisioned Bidding:** Focus on programmatic transparency.
- **UID2:** Identity framework for the open internet.

**Measurement:** 
Focus on hybrid/transparent measurement across the "Open Web" (everything outside of Google/Meta/Amazon).

**Strengths:** 
Neutral approach (not a publisher); massive reach across the open web, CTV, and audio; leadership in industry-standard identity solutions.

**Limitations:** 
Requires higher expertise and higher minimum spends; signal is less "native" than walled gardens.

**Primary Use-cases:** 
Programmatic brand scaling, CTV (Connected TV) advertising, and open-web acquisition.
