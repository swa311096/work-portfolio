# PM Interview Prep

A single place to connect **prep material** with **evidence** from this portfolio — so stories, frameworks, and deep dives stay aligned.

| Layer | Use for interviews |

---

## Prep buckets (outline)

Use the sections below as a checklist; add child pages under this hub when you want notes, drills, or draft answers in one place.

- **Story bank** — 5–8 polished stories from Work (scope, conflict, ambiguity, failure, influence, technical depth).
- **Product sense** — critique flows, prioritize backlogs, design metrics; pair with Projects teardowns and Ads Ecosystem notes.
- **Execution & analytics** — how you ran discovery, shipped, measured, and iterated; tie each bullet to a Work write-up.
- **Estimation & tradeoffs** — back-of-envelope and “build vs buy vs partner” framed with your domain (ads, growth, infra-adjacent PM).
- **Behavioral & leadership** — cross-functional moments, exec communication, coaching — often the same stories as above with different emphasis.
