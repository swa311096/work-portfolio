# Instructor Cost Reduction Initiative

**Company:** Interview Kickstart (VC-backed EdTech Startup)
**Role:** Founding Product Manager, New Products & Research
**Timeline:** Jul 2021 – Mar 2023

## Overview
Led a company-wide initiative to reduce annual instructor costs by building a centralized dashboard to track and cap task hours, and standardizing instructor contracts.

## Impact
- Reduced annual instructor costs by **$1M**

## What I Did
- Built a centralized dashboard to track instructor task hours and enforce caps
- Partnered with Operations to standardize instructor contract clauses
- Led cross-company alignment on the initiative end-to-end

## Skills & Tools
Cost Optimization, Operational Efficiency, Dashboard, Data Analysis, Contract Standardization, Cross-functional Leadership, SQL
