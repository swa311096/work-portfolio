# Sales Funnel Redesign

**Company:** Interview Kickstart (VC-backed EdTech Startup)
**Role:** Founding Product Manager, New Products & Research
**Timeline:** Jul 2021 – Mar 2023

## Overview
Redesigned the sales funnel to introduce instructor-led webinars as a lead engagement mechanism, significantly improving sales conversion.

## Impact
- Improved sales conversion ratio by **5 percentage points**
- Generated **$2M additional revenue**

## What I Did
- Redesigned the top-of-funnel to introduce instructor-led webinars for lead nurturing
- Conducted weekly training sessions for 20+ sales associates
- Partnered with instructors and sales team to align content with conversion goals
